Process and Product Metrics

We track both product and process metrics to measure the quality of our deliverables and the effectiveness of our development process.

Product Metrics

Product metrics help us measure the quality of the code we deliver and track defects throughout the development lifecycle.

Code Coverage

Code Coverage measures the percentage of our code that's tested. It helps us measure the quality of the code we deliver by ensuring our codebase is thoroughly tested.

View Code Coverage

Defect Density

Defect Metrics track bugs across the entire development lifecycle. Every defect goes into our tracking spreadsheet with a status. This lets us know our quality trends and identify areas for improvement.

View Defect Density

Process Metrics

Process metrics help us measure how effectively we're executing as a team and how well we estimate our work.

Sprint Velocity

At the end of each sprint, we compare story points we planned versus what we actually completed. This helps us measure how we're executing as a team and how we estimate future work.

View Sprint Velocity

Burndown Chart

Burndown Charts track remaining work during each sprint. As sprint days progress, the work remaining should also decrease, helping us visualize our progress and identify potential issues early.

View Burndown Chart
